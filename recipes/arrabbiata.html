Arrabbiata

Description

This spicy tomato sauce is often served with penne pasta.

Ingredients

  • canned tomatoes
  • red chili pepper
  • garlic
  • olive oil
  • salt and black pepper

Steps

  1. Chop garlic and chili pepper.
  2. Saute garlic in olive oil.
  3. Add tomatoes, chili pepper, salt and black pepper.
  4. Let it cook.
