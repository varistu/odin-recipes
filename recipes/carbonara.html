Carbonara

Description

This traditional pasta dish does not use cream.

Ingredients

  • pancetta
  • egg
  • pecorino cheese
  • olive oil
  • black pepper
  • spaghetti

Steps

  1. Fry pancetta.
  2. Cook spaghetti.
  3. Add egg to al dente spaghetti while it's still warm and stir.
  4. Add fried pancetta, shredded pecorino and black pepper.
