Chili con carne

Description

This is a stew. Let the flavors develop over night and serve it the next day.

Ingredients

  • canned tomatoes
  • garlic
  • onions
  • red chili peppers
  • ground beef
  • kidney beans
  • olive oil
  • cumin
  • salt and black pepper

Steps

  1. Chop garlic, onions and chili peppers.
  2. Saute garlic and onion in olive oil in a big pot.
  3. Add cumin and stir for a short moment.
  4. Add ground beef and brown it well.
  5. Add tomatoes, chili peppers, salt, black pepper and a glass of water.
  6. Let the stew boil and put it in the oven for 1 hour.
  7. Add kidney beans and put it back in the oven for 30 minutes.
